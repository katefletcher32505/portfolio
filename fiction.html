A collected portfolio of fiction, revision, and reflective writing that shows my engagement with craft, emotional structure, and narrative experimentation.

About the Portfolio

This portfolio brings together fiction writing, revision-based work, and reflective writing about process. It shows how I think through story not just at the level of plot, but through tone, structure, emotional pacing, and atmosphere.

It also reflects one of the strongest through-lines in my writing: my interest in interiority, tension, family dynamics, ambiguity, and the emotional logic underneath what people say and do.

What It Demonstrates

This work demonstrates creative range, sustained revision, and serious engagement with craft. It also shows my ability to reflect critically on my own writing choices, which matters to me not only as a creative writer, but as someone interested in rhetoric, audience, and meaning-making more broadly.
